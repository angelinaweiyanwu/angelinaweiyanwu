The sea gives and the sea takes.

WE died, both YOU and I have returned to the sea. I was initially happy to have found you, but I am now even more grateful for your departure. There are countless fish in the sea, and we did not belong to each other. We have reached a point where we must part ways and embark on separate paths.On these individual journeys, we will grow and eventually find our soulmates. This marks our farewell.
By Angelina Wei Yan Wu
